River day decision

Float first. Rafting TBD.

The relaxed Deschutes float is the easiest all-family memory. Whitewater is still a choice to make together: Sun Country Tours is the short, close option, and Ouzel Outfitters is the alternate.

Float Option

Float the Deschutes

Best for: young kids, Amy who doesn't swim.

Why it fits: lower cost, flexible timing, low stress, and very Sunriver.

Rafting option A

Sun Country Tours · Big Eddy Thriller

Best for: a short, memorable adrenaline adventure without losing the whole day.

Suggested slot: Saturday morning. Keep the afternoon flexible.

Rafting option B

Ouzel Outfitters

Best for: a more outfitter-style rafting adventure or longer river day.

Tradeoff: more planning and commitment than the quick Big Eddy option.
