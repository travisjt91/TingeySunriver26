The itinerary

Six days, built around family energy

Wednesday · August 5

Arrival Among the Pines

Get there, unload, breathe, and make the first night feel easy.

  • Before 3 PM: Travel, grocery stop, lunch on the road, quick Village preview if you arrive early.
  • 3 PM: Check in, unpack, claim beds, set up bikes, snacks, swimsuits, and kid chaos control.
  • Evening: Sunriver Village dinner + Goody’s ice cream + slow family bike loop near the house.

Thursday · August 6

Pool Day + Bike Path Freedom

A classic Sunriver day: bikes, SHARC, naps, and a low-pressure evening.

  • Morning: Easy cruiser ride on the paved Sunriver pathways. Pick a 30–60 minute loop.
  • Afternoon: SHARC swim block. Bring goggles, towels, snacks, and a backup sweatshirt.
  • Evening: Sunriver Brewing or Village dinner, then deer spotting and porch time.

Friday · August 7

Deschutes River Float + Art Fair Night

The river day that feels like a vacation commercial.

  • Morning: Slow breakfast, sunscreen, water shoes, and a dry bag.
  • Midday: Float, kayak, canoe, or paddleboard from Sunriver Marina or a guest-access launch option.
  • Evening: Sunriver Art Fair in the Village, dinner, treats, and a golden-hour walk.
Kid score: 5/5Parent stress: mediumCost: $$$
Sunriver MarinaArt Fair
Saturday · August 8

High Desert Museum + Observatory Night

Animals, history, lava-land learning, then stars.

  • Morning: High Desert Museum. Best for kids when everyone still has energy.
  • Afternoon: Rest, SHARC splash, or short bike ride. Do not over-schedule before stargazing.
  • Night: Sunriver Nature Center & Observatory. Book ahead if a night program is open.
Kid score: 4/5Parent stress: mediumCost: $$$
Museum hoursObservatory
Sunday · August 9

Lava Cave or Benham Falls + Final Village Night

Pick your adventure based on smoke, energy, and reservations.

  • Option A: Lava River Cave if you secured timed entry. Bring warm layers and real lights.
  • Option B: Benham Falls / easy forest walk if you want no-reservation nature.
  • Evening: Final Art Fair stroll, family photos, dinner, ice cream, and partial packing.
