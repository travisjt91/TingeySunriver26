River Day Decision

Float the River vs. Whitewater Rafting

For the whole group, the relaxed river float is the easiest all-family memory. For the Dabbs/adventure crew, Sun Country Tours is the lead whitewater option, with Ouzel Outfitters as the alternate.

Recommended Main Group Plan: Float the Deschutes

Best for: relaxing, kids, grandparents, photos, and everyone moving at their own pace.

Suggested slot: Monday late morning / early afternoon, after the group is settled and before the final checkout morning.

Why it fits: lower cost, flexible timing, low stress, and very Sunriver.

Lead Whitewater Option: Sun Country Tours — Big Eddy Thriller

Best for: a short, memorable adrenaline adventure without losing the whole day.

Suggested slot: Saturday morning. Leave Sunriver around 8:15 AM, check in around 9:00 AM, raft mid-morning, then keep the afternoon flexible.

Why it fits: close to Sunriver/Bend, family-friendly Class I–III excitement, and a manageable time commitment.

Alternate Whitewater Option: Ouzel Outfitters

Best for: a group that wants a bigger outfitter-style rafting adventure or a longer river day.

Tradeoff: likely more planning and commitment than the quick Big Eddy option.
